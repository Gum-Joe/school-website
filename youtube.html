All about me

By Kishan Sambhi

My YouTube Channel

Since Year 5, I have been running the YouTube channel Gum Joe. Before, I used to post very infrequently, but I have recently started picking up the pace of uploads.

My videos:
How I do it:

I shoot all my video on either my iPad (1080p) or my Canon camera (720p). Recently I got myself an iPad tripod stand, so I can have less shanky footage. Once I have shot all my footage, using my PC as a makeshift teleprompter, I then proceed with editing, usually in Adobe Premiere Elements or Adobe Premiere Pro. I use Adobe After Effects for all my animations and Adobe Audition for my audio. Once done, I then export the project as a mp4 H.264 and then publish to YouTube. I get help for the camera and sometimes as an extra person in my videos mainly from my dad.
